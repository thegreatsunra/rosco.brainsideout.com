You'll dig our skills.

A lot of work goes into building a website, so much so that calling yourself a web designer is about as descriptive as calling yourself a mammal.

We certainly are staffed by mammals here at Brainside Out, but how can you know if we are squirrels or humans? In answering that question, let's get a bit more fine-grained as to our skills:

  • Website Design - We are all about pushing pixels and increasing the general hotness of the web. Feel free to check out our Portfolio to see examples from those who have turned us loose.
  • XHTML/CSS Coding - While we love being involved in the architecture and design process for a site, we're also great at "slicing" other peoples' designs into standards-based XHTML and CSS.
  • Copywriting - We're pretty good at putting together, like, coherent sentences that your users may find mildly entertaining. If nothing else, we promise to spell words correctly and keep the cursing to a minimum.
  • Information Architecture - This is a fancy way of saying that we spend a lot of time sketching stuff out on paper, trying to figure out the best way to arrange your website and content so it makes the most sense to your users.

If there remains any doubt, we assure you that our business is free of squirrels. Mostly.
