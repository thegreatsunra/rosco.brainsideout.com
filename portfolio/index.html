Our skill set runs the whole gambit of the internet, from project management, to information architecture, to graphic design, to back-end scripting and such.

We are most comfortable working in a standards-based environment that is steeped in proper XHTML and CSS. On the technical side of things, we prefer using PHP and MySQL to do our dirty work, on a Linux/FreeBSD/*nix hosting platform.

One might wonder how we have come to know and do so much in such a short period of time, having spent barely a quarter-century on this wild, wild planet. Our secret to success is simple: we stay up all night and only sleep while blinking.
