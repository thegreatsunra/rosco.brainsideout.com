Holy wow! Updates!

That's right ya'll, we've made some changes in this here town. Now, for your pixel-hogging pleasure, all desktop wallpapers are available in 1600x1200 resolution. That's like, huge!

Also, aren't watermarks totally dumb? We thought so, so we took them off every photo. Now you can roll around inside your new background without worrying that you might run into a logo or words or something!

Go on, you know you want to say it. We're awesomely awesome.

Just don't be a jerk.

You may use these images for your computer desktop. You may use these images on your co-worker's desktop. You may even use them on your boss's desktop, just so long as you don't use them in a manner that you'd be embarrassed to talk about on Monday.

However, you may not use these images for your own projects, nor may you redistribute them without the permission of Brainside Out. You may not post them in your LiveJournal and be all like "OMG look im th best photographer evar!"

We'll find out about this. Trust us. We have ways.
